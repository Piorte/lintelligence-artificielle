    Presented at the Google I/O conference, the Universal Translator aims to transpose videos from one language to another, while preserving the original intonation and atmosphere. It is actually a lip-synced translator.

    6. Sidekick, the personal assistant for Google Docs :

    13. Can AI think or learn:


    AI involves 3 types of learning:

    • supervised learning
    • unsupervised learning
    • reinforcement learning

    Supervised learning is a computer program that confronts AI with a set of data which have already been used by his training. Thanks to this, the AI will be able to rely on its knowledge to the color of the apple it will be green or red and for that of the orange it will be orange.


    Unsupervised learning is confronting the AI with objects that are not in its database. So the AI will have to learn on its own, for example how many species are there? She never learned but she knows that there are different colors like the hen and the rabbit, so she can conclude that there are 2 different species by their color.


    Reinforcement learning : AlphaGO, Google's AI uses this learning technique. This one implied tell the program what you want it to do, and then let it do its thing. To the extent that as he progresses towards the desired goal, he is better rated and his positive actions are thus reinforced. Eg in chess, the AI learns its moves that cause it to lose and will not use them in the future, but on the other hand the winning moves she retains and uses in the future.

    16. The dangers of AI :


    AI has been able to make great advances in many fields such as medicine, art, etc...
    But in the wrong hands it can be used for criminal purposes or disinformation,
    threats are serious:

    • Impersonation: impersonating someone by making them say or do something that they has ever done (as with make-a-video), and these fake videos will be virtually undetectable. In the United States, a mother received a call from a man who claimed to be holding her daughter and asked for a ransom. The hesitant mother notified the police, a little later it is discovered when the investigation has been solved that the man had used an AI to clone the daughter's voice and trick the mother into thinking he had her daughter hostage and that in truth her daughter is doing very well. The story is terrifying and shows how AI can be used for criminal activities. See the full article from futura-sciences
    • self-driving car hacking: taking control of a vehicle and using it as a weapon. Ex : terrorist attack
    • tailored phishing (phishing): increase the effectiveness of "phishing" aimed at collecting secure information.
    • hacking of AI-controlled systems: disrupting infrastructure, e.g. causing an outage widespread electricity.
    • false information written by the AI: writing propaganda articles for example (chatgpt)
